How to Play

Click on any cell in the 3x3 grid to place your mark (X or O).

Players take turns until one player gets three marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) or all cells are filled (resulting in a draw).

When a player wins or a draw occurs, an alert will be displayed, and the game will reset for a new round.

To manually restart the game, click the "Restart Game" button.

The sidebar shows the results of each game. The most recent game score is displayed at the top of the scores list.

To clear all scores, click the "Clear Scores" button located below the sidebar.
